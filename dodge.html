Dodge

2017

Ever wanted your finger chased by a rotating pizza? Then this is the game for you! The game is deceptively easy but tricky to master. The concept is simple, move your finger to dodge spinning object of destruction. The faster you are the further you can get.

A mobile game that I developed using Unity for the android platform. I had previously published it on the Google Playstore. However, I have since taken it down as it is no longer supported.
