Sling

2016

Avoid incoming obstacles with a ball hanging on to a handle, sling it around to maneuver around the obstacles to see how far you can get, and unlock new balls to show off your achievements.

A mobile game developed using Unity for the android platform. It was previously published on the Google Playstore where it accumulated over 400 downloads. However, it has since been taken down as it is no longer supported.
