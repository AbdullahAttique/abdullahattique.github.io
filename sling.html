Sling

2016

Avoid hitting the incoming obstacles with the ball hanging off your finger. Sling it around to maneuver through the obstacles to see how far you can get, and unlock new balls to show off your achievements.

A mobile game that I developed using Unity for the android platform. I had previously published it on the Google Playstore where it accumulated over 400 downloads. However, I have since taken it down as it is no longer supported.
