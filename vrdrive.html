VR Driving

2017

Speed through traffic without crashing for as long as you can.

This game was developed for the Steam VR platform using Unity. The player can use their motion controller to grab the steering wheel of the vehicle in order to maneuver it through traffic on the highway while traveling at a constant high speed.
